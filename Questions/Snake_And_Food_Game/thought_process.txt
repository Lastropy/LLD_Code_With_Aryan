Snake And Food Game
Actual Problem (DSA) - https://leetcode.com/problems/design-snake-game/

Part 0: Core Idea
- M x N board 
- There is a snake. 
- Snake has length, body, head, tail. Starting length = 1 (let's say)
- A fruit is there.
- If snake eats fruit, it's length increases by 1; fruit respawns elsewhere (non-snake square)
- The player controls snake's direction -> 4 directions -> up, down, left, right
- If snake's head touches its own body or the wall, it dies.
- Game keeps going until snake covers entire grid or dies.
- Score of player in game = # of fruits eaten till now.

Part 1: State Functionalities
- The snake moves in a grid based environment
- The player controls the direction of the head of the snake.
- The snake grows when it eats food.
- The game ends when snake collides with a wall or itself.
